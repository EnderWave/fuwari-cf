#### 证明($MP$广义逆的唯一性)  
设$X$和$Y$都是$MP$广义逆  
$$(1)X=XAX\to col(X)\subseteq col(XA)$$  
又  
$$col(XA)\subset col(X)\to col(X)=col(XA) \to row(XA)\subseteq row(A)$$  
$$(2)A+AYA\to A(I-YA)=0$$  
$$
\begin{align}
&\to col(I-YA)\subseteq ker(A)={x|x\perp row(A)}\\
&\to col(A-YA)\perp col(X)\\
&\to(I-YA)X=0\\
&\to X=YAX
\end{align}
$$  
$$(3)Y=YAY\to row(Y)\subseteq row(AY\to col(AY)\subseteq col(A)$$  
$$(I-AX)A=0\to A^T(I-AX)=0$$  
$$
\begin{align}
&\to col(I-YA)\subseteq ker(A^T)=[row(A^T)]^{\perp}=[col(A)]^{\perp}\\
&\to row(Y)\perp col(I-AX)\\
&\to(I-AX)Y^T=0\to Y(I-AX)=0\\
&\to Y=YAX
\end{align}
$$
